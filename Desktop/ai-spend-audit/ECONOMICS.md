# Unit Economics

## The Credex Opportunity
- **Target Value:** Companies spending >$1,000/mo on AI.
- **Conversion Goal:** 5% of audits result in a "Credex Consultation."
- **Revenue Logic:** Credex makes a margin on bulk-sourced credits. If a user moves $10k of spend to Credex at a 20% discount, Credex captures ~$200-$500 in platform fees.

## Scalability to $1M ARR
To hit $1M ARR in 18 months, the tool must:
1. Generate 5,000 audits/month.
2. Convert 250 high-value leads to the marketplace.
3. Average $333 revenue per conversion.
